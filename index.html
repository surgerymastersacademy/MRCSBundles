About The Exam

Part A: Written Examination

This is a five-hour single best answer (SBA) exam consisting of two papers taken on the same day. It's designed to test your knowledge of applied basic sciences and the principles of surgery in general.

Part B: OSCE

This is a practical exam that assesses your knowledge and skills in a clinical setting. It consists of a series of stations where you'll be tested on clinical examination, communication, and procedural skills.

Typical Exam Diets

Exam dates are set by the colleges and can vary slightly. Always check the official college websites for the most accurate and up-to-date schedules.
